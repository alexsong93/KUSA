Save My Seoul - Jubilee Project

November 8, 2014, 7 PM at White Lecture Hall

Duke Korean Undergraduate Student Association is excited to welcome Jubilee Project for an exclusive advance screening of their newest film, "Save My Seoul"! "Save My Seoul" is a documentary that uncovers the realities of rampant prostitution and sex trafficking in Seoul, South Korea. The film is intended to raise awareness of the issue to others outside of Korea and ultimately spur action to end sex trafficking through a worldwide youth movement. To learn more, visit http://www.savemyseoul.com/.

Korean Barbeque

Duke Korean Undergraduate Student Association presents Korean BBQ! Join us on October 4th at 2:00 PM at the central grills to enjoy some delicious Korean meats, grilled by your KUSA Exec board! The menu so far consists of samgyupsal (pork belly), LA galbi (marinated beef short ribs), and kimchi.

Chuseok Festival

September 13, 2014, 2PM at Keohane Atrium

KUSA and DKSA(Duke Korean Student Association for graduate school students) are going to celebrate Korean national holiday Chuseok! We will be having Korean traditional foods like Jeon and Songpyeon. We also have prepared some icebreaker games too!

It will be a great opportunity to celebrate one of the most celebrated holidays in Korea with KUSA, as well as to network with grad students of DKSA. Hope to see you there!
